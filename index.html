Zombie Apocalypse: Home Defense

Game Description

Amidst the chaos of a zombie outbreak, you play as John, a loving father who returns home from work to find his family under attack. Armed with whatever he can find, John must navigate through his once peaceful home, now infested with the undead.

As you explore room by room, you'll encounter terrifying zombies hungry for flesh. Use your wit and resourcefulness to find weapons, barricade doors, and protect your family at all costs. Time is running out, and every decision could mean life or death.

Will John be able to save his family and survive the night in this intense battle against the zombie horde?

Game Controls: Control 1, Control 2, Control 3
